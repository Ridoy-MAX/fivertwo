Case Studies

'V' from Yorkshire UK

Background

A colleague of 'V's' had become highly attracted to her, he ('J') like 'V', was in an unfulfilling marital relationship. After having several coffees together in the staff canteen during which they each opened up about their home life, empathy became a bonding factor. They fell in love and wanted to be together after several secret meetings and working away together. 'V' had the courage to leave her marital partner and moved into a rented home where she hoped 'J' would join her. 'J' lacked courage to make the break and feared being condemned by his children should he leave his wife for 'V'. It seems like 'J' would never leave his abusive marriage with 'K' so 'V' contacted us.

After an initial consultation in which we offered insight and guidance, 'V' commissioned us to help 'J' to make the break and move in with her. 'J' was being severely abused at home and feared the wrath of his wife whose cruelty was continuing to damage his physical and mental health. Our work was arduous but successful in not not only giving J the courage to make the break and come together with V, but we were able to achieve a greater bond between 'J' and his children who left home around the same time as 'J' as they too had suffered abuse at the hands of their mother ('J's' wife 'K'). 'V' and 'J' are now very happy together and are supportive of 'J's' union with V

'V's Testimonial:

"Through a combination of specialist techniques, you have helped my soul mate to shed his feelings of misplaced guilt and release him from the nightmare he was living in for years. I am overwhelmed by the results. If you are thinking about contacting Love Problem Solver to help you or a loved one make positive changes in your lives, you won't find any other company as dedicated and passionate about their work - they genuinely care and want to help people be who and where they are meant to be in life"

There was rivalry and discord to contend with until all 3 left the base and M returned to the USA whilst 'L' and 'P' flew back to their UK base together where they continued to see each other with M still hot on 'P's' tail and 'P' had to tell his wife he had met someone else and wanted a divorce. M remained in the USA whilst L and P left the armed forces some months later. 'M' flew over the UK to see 'P' and did all possible to push 'L' (our client) out of the life of 'P'. 'L' decided to call us for help.

After several consultations 'L' decided to enlist our help in resolving the complex triangle so that she and P could come together and our work is ongoing presently but 'L' has kindly given us this testimonial on the progress so far:

'L's Testimonial:
